About

MSA Hackathon is the first annual Hackathon brought to you by the UWMSA

Every individual possesses unique skills and ideas. When individuals come together and collaborate, great things can be achieved. One great field for use of new ideas and innovation is giving back to and improving our community. At this event, you'll have the opportunity to learn new skills from leaders in our community, collaborate with others to make something amazing, and of course, the chance to win prizes.

During the weekend of the event, you'll be tasked with creating and developing an idea that has the power to revolutionize your community. You'll have access to workshops where you can learn and hone your skills in tech, business, and other fields. You'll be able to talk to mentors and many other awesome individuals like yourself. Oh, and did we mention the free food and swag?
